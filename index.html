About Us

The overall vision and mission of KOGA faith Foundation is to mobilize faith-based organizations home and abroad to H.E.L.P Africa ( Heal, Empower, Love and Promote Africa). Initiators of Faith-Based organizations in Africa contribute so much sacrificially towards the socio-economic development of the Continent yet are given little or no recognition for their work, One of the main reasons for this is that the misconception between religious institutions and Faith-based organizations and KFF has taken the challenge to sensitize relevant Governmental and non Governmental agencies in Africa trough specialized trainings,workshops,conferences, etc on the differences between Religious institutions and faith-based organizations and on the benefits in working with FBO's in addressing social vices and for socio-economic development.
